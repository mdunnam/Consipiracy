THE Q OPERATION

What Q Claimed

Q identified as a Q-clearance (nuclear/classified access) source with access to the highest levels of US military and intelligence. The drops stated that a group of military and intelligence "white hats" had approached candidate Donald Trump in 2015-16 and asked him to run for president as the public face of their counter-operation against the Cabal — a deep state network of corrupt officials, banking interests, intelligence agency criminals, human traffickers, and foreign actors who had captured US government institutions.

The operation was described as a 10-year plan — beginning with the 2016 election and culminating in mass arrests, military tribunals, public disclosure of the Cabal's crimes, and a fundamental restructuring of US and global governance. The Drops — Structure and Content

The 4,953 Q drops are archived on QAlerts.app and multiple mirror sites. They are structured as interrogative statements, direct assertions, coded references, and photo attachments. Common recurring themes: the Cabal's structure (bloodline families at apex, intelligence agencies as operational layer, media as control mechanism); the scale of child trafficking and adrenochrome harvesting; the financial system reset (NESARA/GESARA); the role of the military as the last uncorrupted institution; the importance of "the plan" proceeding without public panic; and the eventual "Great Awakening" — a mass public disclosure event.
