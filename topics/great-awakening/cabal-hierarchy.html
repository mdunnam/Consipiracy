THE CABAL HIERARCHY

The pyramid of control — from bloodline families at the apex, through secret societies and financial institutions, down to the intelligence agencies, media, and politicians who implement the agenda while most of them have no idea of the level above them.

The Pyramid Structure

The Cabal is not a single organisation. It is a network of networks — connected by shared ideology, mutual financial interest, and in many cases blackmail — operating across multiple institutional layers. Understanding its structure requires mapping the layers rather than looking for a single command centre.

Layer 1 — The Bloodline Families

The apex of the hierarchy: 13-14 bloodline families (Rothschild, Rockefeller, Windsor, Sassoon, Astor, Bundy, Collins, DuPont, Freeman, Kennedy, Li, Onassis, Russell, Van Duyn) who have maintained multigenerational wealth and power through financial control, political marriages, and occult traditions. Their power pre-dates the nation-state.

Layer 2 — The Coordinating Institutions

BIS, Committee of 300, Club of Rome, Council on Foreign Relations, Trilateral Commission, Bilderberg Group, the Vatican. These institutions coordinate policy across the bloodline families' various national and corporate interests and ensure alignment among the next layer down.

Layer 3 — National Institutions & Corporations

Central banks, intelligence agencies (CIA/MI6/Mossad/BND), major media corporations, pharmaceutical companies, defence contractors, and the legislative/executive branches of captured governments. These institutions implement policy without necessarily knowing the full agenda above them.

Layer 4 — Operational Actors

Politicians, journalists, academics, NGO heads, think-tank analysts, corporate executives, and regulatory officials who implement the Cabal's agenda — believing they are pursuing legitimate careers or genuine ideals — while unknowingly serving the layer above.

The Control Mechanisms

The Cabal maintains control through three primary mechanisms that work across all layers:

01

Financial Control

The debt-based monetary system ensures that governments, corporations, and individuals are structurally dependent on Cabal-owned financial institutions. Any significant actor who opposes the Cabal can be isolated from capital, credit, and investment — effectively neutralised economically.

02

Blackmail & Kompromat

The Epstein/Maxwell network was the most visible arm of a systematic kompromat operation: compromising politicians, executives, royals, and celebrities through filmed sexual crimes, then using the material to ensure compliance. GA researchers believe this system encompasses far more individuals than the documented Epstein case — it is the Cabal's primary compliance mechanism for its Layer 3 and 4 operators. 03

Narrative Control

Mockingbird media, academia, and education systems ensure that the general population believes the Cabal's preferred version of reality. Anyone who questions this reality is labelled, marginalised, and if necessary destroyed professionally. The "conspiracy theory" label — a CIA invention — is the primary normalisation tool. The Hierarchy's Weakness

The pyramid structure that makes the Cabal powerful also makes it vulnerable: control is maintained through secrecy. The Great Awakening operates by exposing the hierarchy — not by fighting it at the bottom (where it is strongest) but by disclosing it from the top, destroying the secrecy that all layers depend on. "Conspiracy theorists" examining Rothschild banking, CIA coups, and Mockingbird media are not marginal cranks — they are the existential threat to the most powerful system of control ever constructed.
