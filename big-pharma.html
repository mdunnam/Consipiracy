BIG PHARMA & MEDICAL CONTROL

Medicine was industrialised to manage disease, not cure it. Every chronic illness is a revenue stream — and the cures already exist.

The Rockefeller Medicine Takeover

In 1910, the Flexner Report — commissioned by John D. Rockefeller and Andrew Carnegie — systematically destroyed natural medicine schools across America, replaced them with petrochemical pharmacology, and created the modern pharmaceutical paradigm: treat symptoms, manage illness, never cure. Within two decades, over half of the natural medicine schools that existed in 1900 had been shut down. The ones that remained were remodelled around synthetic, patentable compounds.

Rockefeller Standard Oil chemists discovered that petroleum byproducts could be reformulated as drugs. Unlike plants — which cannot be patented — synthetic molecules can. A patented drug generates indefinite royalty income. A natural cure does not. This single economic reality explains the entire modern pharmaceutical industry.

Key Mechanism

You cannot patent a vitamin, mineral, or plant extract. You can patent a synthetic molecule. The pharmaceutical industry's opposition to natural medicine is not about efficacy — it is about intellectual property.

Suppressed Cancer Cures

Royal Rife's Universal Microscope in the 1930s observed living cancer cells at frequencies that destroyed them without harming surrounding tissue. His 1934 clinical trials showed a 100% remission rate in 16 terminally ill patients. The American Medical Association moved to suppress his work. His laboratory was raided; his equipment destroyed; his research partner was bribed. Rife died in poverty. His technology was buried for nearly 50 years.

This pattern repeats throughout the 20th century: Dr. Max Gerson's nutritional cancer therapy, Dr. Stanislaw Burzynski's antineoplaston treatment, Dr. Tullio Simoncini's bicarbonate protocol, and dozens of others — all demonstrated results, all faced sustained institutional suppression, legal attack, and personal destruction.

The global cancer industry generates over $200 billion annually. A one-time cure eliminates this revenue stream entirely. The incentive structure is not to cure cancer — it is to manage it indefinitely.

Vaccine Safety — The Untold Science

The 1986 National Childhood Vaccine Injury Act granted pharmaceutical manufacturers complete liability immunity for all childhood vaccines. No other product category in American commerce has this protection. If a vaccine injures or kills a child, the manufacturer faces zero civil liability. All claims go to the Vaccine Injury Compensation Fund — a government compensation system funded by a vaccine excise tax — not by the manufacturer.

The mRNA platform deployed in 2021 for COVID-19 was not a traditional vaccine. It instructed human cells to produce the spike protein of SARS-CoV-2 — a protein subsequently identified in peer-reviewed research as independently toxic to the cardiovascular system, capable of crossing the blood-brain barrier, and associated with IgG4 class switching that may suppress normal immune response on repeated dosing.

VAERS Data

The US Vaccine Adverse Event Reporting System (VAERS) recorded more adverse event reports for the COVID-19 vaccines in 2021 alone than for all other vaccines combined in the previous 30 years. VAERS is known to capture between 1% and 10% of actual adverse events.

Fluoride — Industrial Waste in the Water Supply

Water fluoridation was initiated in 1945, promoted as a dental health measure. Fluoride — a byproduct of phosphate fertiliser manufacturing and aluminium smelting — is classified as a neurotoxin by multiple peer-reviewed studies. A 2012 Harvard meta-analysis of 27 studies found consistent association between fluoride exposure and lowered IQ in children. The pineal gland — which regulates sleep cycles, endocrine function, and in metaphysical traditions is associated with third-eye perception — calcifies at significantly higher rates in fluoridated populations.

The Weaponised Food Supply

Glyphosate — Monsanto/Bayer's herbicide — is now found in 80% of non-organic food products in the US, in breast milk, in umbilical cord blood, and in rainfall. It is classified as a probable carcinogen by the World Health Organisation's IARC. It disrupts the gut microbiome, which is now understood to regulate immune function, mental health, and neurological development. The mass adoption of ultra-processed food, seed oils oxidised at high temperatures, and high-fructose corn syrup has created an unprecedented chronic disease epidemic — every component of which generates pharmaceutical revenue.
