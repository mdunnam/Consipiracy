FALSE FLAGS & STAGED EVENTS

Governments have staged attacks on their own citizens to justify wars, gun control, and expanded surveillance. The documents proving it are public record.

What Is a False Flag?

A "false flag" operation is an attack staged by one party but designed to appear as if it was carried out by another. The term originates in naval warfare, where ships flew the flag of a foreign nation before attacking, to assign blame to an enemy and create a pretext for war. In modern context, false flag operations are used to justify pre-planned military campaigns, legislative changes, and expansions of state power that would otherwise be politically impossible to achieve without a manufactured crisis.

This is not a theory. Operation Northwoods — the 1962 U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff proposal to stage terrorist attacks on American citizens and blame Cuba — was signed by every member of the Joint Chiefs. It was submitted to President Kennedy. Kennedy refused to sign it. The document was classified until 1997 and is publicly available through the National Security Archive.

The Pattern

Every major false flag follows the same structure: (1) Event occurs. (2) Identity of perpetrators announced within hours, before investigation. (3) Pre-written legislation is introduced. (4) Alternative explanations are labelled dangerous and censored. (5) Physical evidence that contradicts the official narrative disappears.

September 11, 2001 — The Defining Event

World Trade Center Building 7 — a 47-storey steel-framed skyscraper — collapsed at 5:20 PM on September 11, 2001. No aircraft struck it. The official explanation attributed its collapse to office fires. Over 3,600 architects and engineers — under the organisation Architects & Engineers for 9/11 Truth — have produced a peer-reviewed analysis demonstrating that Building 7's collapse, at near-free-fall acceleration into its own footprint with characteristic squib patterns and molten steel pooling, is physically consistent only with controlled demolition.

The NIST (National Institute of Standards and Technology) final report on WTC7, released in 2008, relied on a computer model that was never made available for independent peer review, and acknowledged a 2.25-second period of free-fall acceleration that was initially denied and then quietly confirmed. Free-fall through a steel structure is physically impossible without simultaneous removal of structural support at multiple points.

The Project for a New American Century (PNAC) — whose signatories included Dick Cheney, Donald Rumsfeld, Paul Wolfowitz, and Jeb Bush — published a document in September 2000 calling for a "transformation of the United States military" that would be slow "absent some catastrophic and catalyzing event — like a new Pearl Harbor." September 11, 2001 was that event.

JFK — The First Modern False Flag

The assassination of President John F. Kennedy on November 22, 1963 remains the most documented and most contested event in modern American political history. The Warren Commission's "lone gunman" conclusion — that Lee Harvey Oswald, acting alone and motivated by personal ideology, fired a single bullet through two men at impossible trajectory angles — was contradicted at the time by medical and physical evidence and has not withstood 60 years of independent forensic scrutiny.

The House Select Committee on Assassinations, in 1979, concluded that Kennedy was "probably assassinated as a result of a conspiracy." Thousands of JFK-related documents remain classified as of 2026, under national security exemptions — despite the passage of 62 years. Kennedy had, in the weeks before his death, signed Executive Order 11110, instructing the U.S. Treasury to issue silver-backed certificates — effectively challenging the Federal Reserve's monopoly on currency issuance.

Staged Mass Shootings — The Gun Grab Template

Every major mass shooting event in the United States has been followed, within hours, by Congressional calls for specific gun control legislation that was already drafted and awaiting introduction. The legislative language does not require weeks of drafting — it exists before the event. The shooting is the trigger, not the cause. This pattern has repeated consistently since the mid-1990s.

Anomalies in multiple events — inconsistent witness accounts, security footage that disappears, forensic evidence that contradicts official timelines, crisis actors with documented histories, and law enforcement response failures that defy probability — have led significant numbers of independent researchers to conclude that at least some of these events were either staged entirely or involved pre-positioned assets that allowed events to proceed while ensuring the maximum political impact.
